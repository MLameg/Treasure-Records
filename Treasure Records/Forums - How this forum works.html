How this forum works
"admin username and profile pic"

1. Click the Register button.

2. Enter your user information and submit your information. Please remember that your "Preferred Name" is what other users will see when you create a post. Your "Username" is what you will use to login to the forum.

3. Follow the links provided to take you back to the main forum page.

4. Open a topic that interests you. If you wish to speak to a Treasure Records representative, there is a contact us page on the main site or post your question in Technical Support. If you have a vinyl record or item relating to vinyl records that you wish to sell or trade, there is a looking to sell/trade discussion. If you have a question pertaining to vinyl records or item relating to vinyl records that you wish to ask other users about, check the "anything and everything..." discussions.

5. Click "Add Post".

6. Enter in your question.

7. Click "Post" to make your question live.

8. If you wish to edit or delete your posts, simply click the "Edit Post" or "Delete Post" buttons.

9. If you wish to send a private message to another user, click the "Private Message" button. The response to your message will appear in your Mailbox.
